# Use architecture decision records

* Status: Accepted

## Problem and Motivation

ADRs are a useful record of decisions made while developing software. In this case they will serve to describe to others
what decisions were made and why.

## Decision

Architectural decisions will be written into Architecture Decision Records.

## Consequences

Over the long term, this will enable new contributors to come up to speed with the thinking behind major aspects of the
architecture of bobotw.

## Rationale

Documentation makes it easier to evict ideas from my head, making room for new ideas.
